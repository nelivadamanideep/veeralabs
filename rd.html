Research & Development

Laboratory-driven formulation and analytical development.

Dosages Expertise

Veera Labs develops a broad range of dosage forms including oral solids, liquids, topicals and injectables, as well as niche and advanced delivery platforms.

Oral Solids

IR tablets & capsules, micro-tabs in capsules, DR/ER, ODTs, pellets, effervescent, lozenges, sublingual, oral thin films and sprays.

Liquids & Softgels

Liquid suspensions (deflocculated), nano-emulsions, self-emulsifying systems, and soft gelatin platforms.

Topicals & Injectables

Creams, ointments, emulgels, liposomal formulations, emulsion-based injectables and peptidyl injectables.

Platform Technologies

Lipid-based delivery, bioavailability enhancement platforms, cyclodextrin complexation, and metered-dose nasal sprays.

Formulation & Analytical Development Lab

Comprehensive formulation design and analytical method development delivered with stability testing, validation, and documentation to meet regulatory expectations.

R&D Equipment & Capacities

  • HPLC / UHPLC and LC-MS systems
  • GC-MS instrumentation
  • Dissolution testers and stability chambers
  • Particle size, zeta potential and in-process analytical tools
  • Sterile processing capability for parenteral prototypes
